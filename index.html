About VK Sports

The Heartbeat of Ajmer Cricket

VK Sports Ajmer is where the city's passion for cricket comes alive. For years we've organised fiercely competitive local tournaments that bring together the best teams and rising talent from across Ajmer and nearby towns.

From nail-biting last-over finishes to floodlit night matches, every tournament is run with professional umpires, transparent scheduling and real cash prizes. Whether you captain a full squad or want to join one as an individual player — this is your arena.
